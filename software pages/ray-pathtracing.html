This is an explaination of Ray and Path Tracing on Nvidia cards, specifically on 50 series cards.

Raytracing and Path Tracing

Raytracing and Path Tracing are both rendering techniques that aim to simulate realistic lighting in games. While runnable on most relatively modern day cards (>2016), Nvidia cards, especially 40 and 50 series Nvidia cards, are much better at calculating the light scenes necessary for ray/path tracing. In combination with other excellent Nvidia features, like their upscaler and multi-frame generation, ray/path tracing are possible for the 5070, even at 4k.
