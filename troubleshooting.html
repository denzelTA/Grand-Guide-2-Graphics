Troubleshooting

Common Fixes

  • Ensure graphics card is properly plugged in
  • Ensure fans have no debris
  • update drivers via the respective application
  • test another PCI input on PSU

Advanced

  • Clean drivers via respective application
  • Reinstall the card
  • Check for any signs of damage or overheating
  • Ensure GPU dosent "sag" (is it properly latched ?)
  • Ensure the PSU (power supply) provides enough wattage for the card

If your problem persists, submit a request to your Graphics Card manufacturer for further assistance with all fixes tried .
